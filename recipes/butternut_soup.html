Butternut soup

Description

This recipe can perfectly be your side dish if you're going for the broccoli quiche ! This soup is really tasty, and is a sweet way to say goodbye to summer and a warm welcome to automn. Trust me, I'm an automn kinda girl. So yeah, it's a really easy and comforting soup.

You'll be happy to notice that you do not have to do any metric conversions here, YAY !

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili powder (sweet or spicy, do what you prefer)
  • 1 stock cube

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven at 200Â°C.
  2. Peel and chop all of your vegetables and spread in an oven-fit dish.
  3. Smear your vegetables with some olive oil.
  4. Add some salt, pepper and chili.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Prepare a broth with your stock cube.
  7. When your vegetables are cooked, add some broth (adjust quantity for the consistence you like), and blend your mix until you have a nice soup.
  8. You just have to enjoy now !
