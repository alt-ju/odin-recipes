Cookies

Description

This recipe is one of my favorite. I have a sweet tooth so, obviously, the first recipe on the first page I'm creating is a cookie recipe.

You'll see that the ingredients measures are in grams, sorry if you're American, I know metric system is not your cup of tea. Hope you will enjoy my cookies though.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 85g butter
  • 100g chocolate chunks
  • 7g baking powder
  • 85g sugar
  • 150g flour
  • 15g vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Soften the butter at room temperature. In a bowl stir it with sugar.
  2. Add the egg and the vanilla sugar.
  3. Slowly pour the flour, baking powder, salt and the chocolate chunks. Stir.
  4. Shape the future cookies with your hands or two spoons and space them on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake at 180Â°C for 8 to 10 minutes, until the edges start to get a brown color.
  6. Let them cool down (I know it's hard) and enjoy !
